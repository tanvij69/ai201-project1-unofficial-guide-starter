⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Best value dining hall on campus by far. For only $5, it's all you can eat. The food quality isn't necessarily the #1 on campus, but it's still really good. There's several different stations offering a range of food, including (but not limited to): Asian (rotates between Korean, Thai, Indian, and Viet), pizza & pasta, sliders & fries, a salad bar, dessert bar, and Soup of the Day. My favorite station is the yogurt parfait section where there's a bunch of toppings to add to your parfait, like blueberries, peaches, granola, and more.
