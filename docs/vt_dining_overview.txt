Virginia Tech Dining Services - Official Overview
Source: dining.vt.edu

Welcome
Dining Services strives to enhance the student experience at Virginia Tech. Our award-winning program offers a variety of venues from all-you-care-to-eat to our own food trucks, and we have dining plan options to fit the needs of any student. Whether you're grabbing a meal between classes, enjoying weekend brunch with your friends, or need a late-night study snack, we can't wait to serve you!

Special Events
What makes Virginia Tech Dining Services unique? Our extensive calendar of special events is just one part of the answer! From annual events like Chili Challenge (going on 32 years!), to pop-up chef features like Chicken Slider Day, to celebrating sustainability with Earth Week, VT Dining Services offers a variety of events each semester that highlight our culinary team in fun and exciting ways.

Nutrition
If you'd like to learn about navigating our dining centers with food allergies, are curious about plant-forward options on campus, or just want to find your favorite foods, our on-staff registered dietitians can help you out!

Sustainability
From locally and sustainably sourced ingredients, to compostable and reusable packaging options, to Homefield Farm, Virginia Tech Dining Services is committed to sustainability.

Student Employment
During your time as a Virginia Tech student, you can get to know another side of campus by working for Dining Services! If you're looking for student employment that will help you gain leadership experience, make new friends, and enjoy some of the best college food in the country, our flexible schedules and dynamic environment will be perfect for you.
